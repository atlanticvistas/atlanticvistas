Facilities - West Cork Holiday Home

You have the entire cottage to yourself with a separate entrance with plenty of parking and privacy. At the west side of the cottage the patio area is a lovely sun trap during the summer months; making it suitable for outdoor dining with BBQ and relaxing furniture.

The cottage is newly refurbished inside and outside. From wraparound insulation and high thermal efficiency windows throughout, new plumbing and electric heating, shower and hot water.

Downstairs is converted into an open-plan kitchen/dining/sitting room next to the bathroom. Upstairs there are two bedrooms. The main bedroom is suitable for two adults while the second bedroom’s bunk bed can sleep up to 3-4 children.

The cottage has amazing views over the Atlantic Ocean, so is called “Atlantic Vistas”.
